Travel details

Getting to San Francisco

Here are the three major airports in the Bay Area with international airline service. SFO is about 13 miles south of downtown; OAK is 5 miles south of downtown; and SJC is in the city of San Jose about 45 miles southwest of downtown San Francisco.

  • San Francisco International Airport (SFO)
    12.5 miles
  • Oakland International Airport (OAK)
    20 miles
  • San Jose International Airport (SJC)
    46 miles
Airport Shuttle

Super Shuttle International is offering a discount to all Google I/O attendees. Reservations must be booked in advance either online or by calling 1-800-BLUE-VAN (1-800-258-3826). The discount code for Google I/O is G66Z7.
